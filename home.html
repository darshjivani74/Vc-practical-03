SINDUR OPERATION

              
              Operation Sindoor was a military operation conducted by the Indian Armed 
            Forces in May 2025, targeting terrorist infrastructure in Pakistan and 
            Pakistan-occupied Kashmir. It was a response to the Pahalgam terrorist 
            attack where 25 tourists were killed. The operation aimed to dismantle 
            terrorist camps and training facilities used to launch attacks against 
            India.
